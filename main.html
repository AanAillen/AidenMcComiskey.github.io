Dual Telescope Mounting Plate

An astrophotographer approached me to design a custom aluminum mounting plate to hold two telescopes simultaneously. Using Fusion 360, I created a durable and precise solution that allows both telescopes to attach securely to his mount, enabling simultaneous astrophotography with both instruments.

Transformer Model for a
Brain Computer Interface (BCI)

Working with data provided by Professor Joseph Makin from Purdue University, I worked on improving BCIs that restored communication to patients who had a disease or an accident. Applying a Transformer model to the data rather than the previously used RNN proved vey succesful, allowing me to decrease the training time from 8 hours to 15 minutes while maintaining roughly the same accuracy (95%). This change allowed the BCI to be trained on the patients as they are using it, enabling live-personalisation of the translator. I submitted this research to the Regeneron WESEF Science Fair, and earned second in Computation Biology.
